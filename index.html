INTRODUCTION

Wizarding World, formerly known as Pottermore, is an official Harry Potter website made by Warner Brothers as part of the Wizarding World franchise. Wizarding World is similar to Pottermore in some ways - for example, you can still be sorted into your Hogwarts house, discover your Patronus, or learn about your wand, but there are many new features, like favourites, where you pick your favourite things about the Wizarding World and can share your choices with the Wizarding World community.
